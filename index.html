EnDIYronment

EnDIYronment is a startup company founded by four girls who love coding & want to take intiative when it come to protecting the environment. Our goal is to give people an incentive to want to reduce their impact on the Earth. Our incentive is proposing DIYs to people that use materials that would normally go in landfills to achieve the crafts.
